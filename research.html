IntelliNet is an innovative research center in Computer Science Department, FAST-National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, Karachi, Pakistan. We believe that the future is filled with smart devices and networks, which not only will facilitate but also create numerous research opportunities. Here in IntelliNet, we develop intelligent systems for Next Generation Networks like Internet of Things, Name Data Networks, 5G Networks, etc. Major research areas are:

Internet of Things (IoTs) Smart Systems (Smart Health-Care, Smart City, Smart Grid, etc.) Name Data Networks/ Information Centric Networks 5G Wireless Networks
Machine-to-Machine Communications Device-to-Device Communications Security and Privacy Blockchain

HEC SRGP Project: Smart Electricity Cloud for Efficient Utilization of Distributed Renewable Sources using Internet of Things



We believe that the energy crisis is affecting the life of a normal user and monopoly of electric suppliers increases the user frustration. We envision a system, where electrical sources are geographically distributed and centrally monitored for maintenance and on-demand sharing.
